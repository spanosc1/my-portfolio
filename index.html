About me

I am a recent graduate of The College of New Jersey with a bachelors degree in computer science and a minor in physics. I entered as a physics major, but soon realized that it was not what I was looking for. So I switched over to computer science, which I greatly enjoy. I am looking to gain more experience in web development. Some of my hobbies include playing video games and watching movies. I was born in Staten Island but I grew up in Jackson, New Jersey, where I spent my summers at Six Flags with my friends. By the way, that's me on the right.

I started to gain an interest in web development by trying out a wide variety of different types of coding. Web development seemed to come the most naturally to me and I enjoy the instant gratification I get from seeing the results of my work right away. One day down the road, I hope to be able to enter the game development field as well as game development has always interseted me.
